info About The Venue

The Grand Hall is a premier destination for concerts, corporate events, and private galas. Featuring state-of-the-art acoustics and a capacity of 2,000, we provide an unforgettable experience for every guest. Our team is dedicated to bringing your vision to life with precision and flair.

Located in the heart of the city, we offer easy access to public transportation and ample parking. The venue includes a main hall, VIP lounges, and full backstage amenities for artists and crew.
